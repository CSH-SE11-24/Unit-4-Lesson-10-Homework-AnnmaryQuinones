Wild Cats

Puma

Pumas color ranges from a light buff to a dark reddish brown. The back of the ears and the tip of the tail are both black. The head is small and broad with a high, arched forehead. They have a slender, elongated body with powerful limbs.

Cheetah

Cheetahs have slender, long-legged bodies with blunt, semi-retractable claws. Their heads are small with high-set eyes. A black tear mark runs from the inner corner of each eye down to the mouth.
